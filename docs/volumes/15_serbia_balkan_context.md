# 15 Serbia/Balkan Context

This source volume defines the intended structure for the generated
Serbia/Balkan context planning checklist.

It is not legal advice, not a permit application, not an approval, and not a
substitute for licensed Serbian/Balkan professionals or local municipality
confirmation. The generated volume must use placeholders and action checklists
from `config/regulatory_placeholders_serbia.yaml`.

Required topics:

1. Permitting process placeholder
2. Need for licensed designer
3. Need for geodetic survey
4. Need for geotechnical report
5. Utility connection placeholders
6. Wastewater approval placeholders
7. Fire safety review placeholder
8. Energy efficiency certificate placeholder
9. Contractor quote process
10. Site supervision requirement
11. Municipality-specific variation warning

Do not cite specific laws unless verified legal text is supplied by the user and
reviewed by qualified local professionals.

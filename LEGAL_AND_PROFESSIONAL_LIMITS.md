# Legal and Professional Limits

Mountain Retreat X1 produces preliminary planning materials only.

## Not Approved Documents

Generated documents are not:

- construction documents
- permit documents
- signed or sealed engineering calculations
- architectural approvals
- structural approvals
- electrical approvals
- mechanical or plumbing approvals
- fire-safety approvals
- wastewater approvals
- legal approvals
- procurement instructions
- financing documents

## Required Review

Before any real-world use, generated materials must be reviewed, corrected, and replaced as needed by appropriately licensed professionals and local authorities.

Required reviewers may include:

- licensed architect
- licensed structural engineer
- licensed electrical engineer or electrician
- licensed mechanical/plumbing engineer or contractor
- geotechnical engineer
- surveyor
- fire-safety/code professional
- local permitting authority
- utility provider
- qualified contractor and cost estimator

## Engineering Limits

The repository must not claim that structural calculations are final. It must not generate stamped, signed, sealed, or legally binding engineering documents. Snow, wind, seismic, foundation, terrace, retaining, connection, and structural member assumptions are placeholders until reviewed by licensed professionals.

## Pricing Limits

Costs are static planning assumptions stored in YAML. The system must not scrape live prices and must not present placeholder prices as supplier quotes, market quotes, contractor bids, or procurement approvals.

## Drawing Limits

SVG drawings are schematic planning drawings only. They are not CAD drawings, permit drawings, shop drawings, fabrication drawings, or construction drawings.

## Use Restriction

Do not use generated documents for construction, permitting, procurement, financing, or legal reliance. Use them only as preliminary planning and coordination aids before professional review.

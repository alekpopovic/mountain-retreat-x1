# Legal and Professional Limits

Mountain Retreat X1 produces preliminary planning materials only.

Generated documents are not:

- construction documents
- permit documents
- signed or sealed engineering calculations
- architectural approvals
- structural approvals
- electrical approvals
- mechanical approvals
- legal approvals
- procurement instructions

Before any real-world use, materials must be reviewed and adapted by appropriately licensed professionals and local authorities.

The repository must avoid language that implies approval, certification, permitting, or professional responsibility.
